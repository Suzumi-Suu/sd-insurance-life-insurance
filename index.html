Types of Life Insurance Coverage

At SD Insurance, we offer various types of life insurance coverage to ensure you are fully protected:

  • Term Life Insurance: Provides coverage for a specific period of time.
  • Whole Life Insurance: Offers lifelong coverage with a savings component.
  • Universal Life Insurance: Combines flexible premiums with investment options.
  • Critical Illness Insurance: Covers major illnesses and medical conditions.

Benefits of Life Insurance

Life insurance provides essential coverage, protecting your family against a wide range of risks, including:
